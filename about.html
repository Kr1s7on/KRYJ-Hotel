    About Us

    KRYJ is a luxury hotel located in the heart of Prague, Czech Republic. Our hotel is designed to provide comfort and relaxation to our guests. We offer a variety of rooms, dining options, and facilities to cater to your needs. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service to ensure that your stay is memorable.

    Establishment of the Original Building

    1890

    The building that now houses KRYJ Boutique Hotel was originally constructed in 1890, serving as a grand townhouse for a prominent Czech family.

    Transformation into a Hotel

    1938

    During the interwar period, the townhouse was converted into a hotel, welcoming guests from across Europe and establishing itself as a symbol of Prague's hospitality.

    Nationalization

    1950

    In the aftermath of World War II, the hotel was nationalized and operated under state ownership, catering to visitors from the Eastern Bloc.

    Privatization and Renovation

    1991

    Following the Velvet Revolution and the end of communist rule, the hotel was privatized and underwent extensive renovations to restore its historic charm while updating its facilities.

    Reopening as KRYJ Boutique Hotel

    2010

    After years of meticulous restoration and modernization, the hotel reopened its doors as KRYJ Boutique Hotel, combining the elegance of its historic past with the luxury of contemporary design.
