    Find the answers to the most frequently asked questions below

    What are the check-in and check-out times?

    Check-in: 3:00 PM
    Check-out: 11:00 AM

    Is parking available at the hotel?

    Yes, we offer complimentary parking for all hotel guests. Valet parking services are also available for an additional fee.

    Does the hotel offer free Wi-Fi?

    Yes, we provide free high-speed Wi-Fi access in all rooms and public areas of the hotel.

    Are there dining options available at the hotel?

    Yes, our hotel features a variety of dining options including a restaurant, a cafe, and room service.

    Does the hotel have fitness facilities?

    Yes, we have a fully equipped fitness center that is open 24/7 for our guests.

    Is the hotel pet-friendly?

    Yes, we welcome pets in designated rooms. Please contact us in advance to ensure availability.

    What payment options are available?

    We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payments. Additionally, guests can choose to pay in cash upon check-in.

    What is the refund policy?

    Refunds are available for cancellations made at least 24 hours before check-in. Please contact our front desk for further assistance with cancellations and refunds.
